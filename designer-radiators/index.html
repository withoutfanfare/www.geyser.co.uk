                  Designer Radiators

                  Designer Radiators are a designed to effectively heat your rooms, much like standard white radiators, but have the added advantage of looking stylish and modern to complement the design of your interiors. With designer radiators you no longer need to think of inventive ways of disguising your radiator or shielding it from view. Designer radiators are something to show off, adding a great focal point to you rooms and you can pick a style that complements your interior design style.

                  Wide Range of Designer Radiator Styles

                  At Geyser we offer a huge selection of styles to meet the needs of every home, from traditional column radiators with modern finishes to ultra-modern aluminium radiators, we pride ourselves on offering the latest designer radiator styles at an affordable price.

                  Radiators with your home in mind

                  Our designer radiator ranges have been specifically designed to consider all types of homes. We have sizes that will effectively heat large open spaces and ones perfect for small box-rooms; Slimline and space-saving radiators that can be fitted behind doors or along narrow corridors; vertical designer radiators that free up wall space for furniture; mirrored radiators for bedrooms and dressing rooms; narrow designer radiators that can fit beside windows or on a narrow wall. Our designer radiators open up possibilities for new radiator placements giving you more options on what you can do with your spaces.

                  High Heat Output Designer Radiators

                  At Geyser.co.uk we have a good selection of high heat output designer radiators that are perfect for large rooms or open plan living-kitchens. We have a wide range of aluminium radiator ranges which boast impressive heat outputs as well as fast heat-up times due to their highly conductive nature.

                  Fast UK delivery, get yours today

                  With our easy-to-use website, simple checkout and Fast UK Delivery, you can have designer heating in your home as soon as next day! it’s never been easier to update your heating and add some luxury to your home. If you have any questions or need any advise before ordering don't hesitate to contact our friendly sales team by phone who can answer any questions you may have.

                  Go ahead... treat yourself to a luxury designer radiator or towel rail today.
