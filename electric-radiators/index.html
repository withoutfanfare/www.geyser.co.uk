                  Electric Radiators

                  Electric energy is the ideal heating solution for anyone looking for an energy-efficient or eco-friendly alternative to central heating or simply for any home or office without gas central heating. Electric heating gives you more control over energy consumption and bills.

                  At Geyser.co.uk, we have an excellent selection of designer electric radiators, we understand that electric heating doesn't have to mean ugly heating. At Geyser we offer the very best in designer electric heating meaning your home can be both efficient and stylish.

                  Our electric heating range includes a wide selection of modern and contemporary electric radiators in both vertical and horizontal sizes. A wide array of colours and finishes including stainless steel, chrome, gloss painted and matt textured powder coated finishes. With plenty of colours to choose from and all at an affordable price. Available in a huge range of styles and sizes, there is an electric heater for any and every room in your home.

                  Are electric radiators energy-efficient?

                  Electric radiators operate at 100 per cent efficiency as all the energy used by the radiator is turned into heat making electric heating less wasteful and more environmentally friendly. As the world makes more steps towards becoming a sustainable electricity generation and we move into a more green focused world it's predicted electricity could be more affordable and plentiful in the future.

                  Are electric radiators expensive to run?

                  As we know Electric Radiators are much less wasteful than traditional central heating as all the energy used is turned into heating your radiator but electric radiators also give you more control over your heating which can reduce costs. Our Ecodesign Lot 20 compliant thermostatic and dynamic heating elements give you even more control as they include an assortment of energy saving features. Features include 24/7 digital programming, open window detection, ambient room temperature and more, all designed to save energy and cut your monthly bills.
