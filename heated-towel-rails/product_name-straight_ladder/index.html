                  Heated Towel Rails

                  At Geyser we believe that you and your bathroom deserve the best, that’s why all of our central heated towel rails offer the best in luxury and design while providing you with that much loved warm and toasty feeling. Our heated towel rails and towel radiators are available in a wide range of styles, sizes and finishes; from standard ladder rails, in range of colours and finishes, to flat panel and striking ultra-modern designer style towel warmers.

                  Luxury Designer Towel Rails

                  At Geyser.co.uk we specialise in bringing you the latest in designer heating at an affordable price. Our luxury range of designer central heated towel rails give your bathroom or cloakroom a real hotel like luxury, whether you only need to heat a small cloakroom or en-suite or a much larger bathroom.

                  Worried about Wall Space?

                  we've got you covered... our ranges include innovative solutions for those with limited and awkward wall space and at Geyser It's not all about style, our designs also carefully consider the needs of individual homes, from busy family homes to studio apartments. For busy bathrooms we have designs that offer lots of towel storage space so you'll no longer be fighting over towels and for small bathrooms or en-suites we have compact heated towel rails that will deliver outstanding warmth.

                  A Wide Range of Central Heating Towel Rail Choices

                  With designs to suit all homes we have a huge choice of finishes to choose from including; matt textured powder finishes, chrome, stainless steel and brushed nickel. We also have a huge variety of colour options including black, white, anthracite, light grey, dark grey and sand brown. All of towel rails come with substantial guarantees.

                  With our easy-to-use website, simple checkout and Fast UK Delivery, you can have designer heating in your home as soon as next day! it’s never been easier to update your heating and add some luxury to your home. If you have any questions or need any advise before ordering don't hesitate to contact our friendly sales team by phone who can answer any questions you may have.
